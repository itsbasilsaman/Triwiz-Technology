Faq

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is digital marketing?

Digital marketing refers to the use of platforms and digital channels to promote products or services. This includes strategies like search engine optimization.

2. Why is digital marketing important for businesses?

Digital marketing allows businesses to reach a wider audience, improve engagement, and increase sales by leveraging online platforms.

3. What is the difference between SEO and PPC?

SEO focuses on organic traffic by optimizing content for search engines, while PPC involves paid advertising to get immediate visibility.

4. What is the role of social media in digital marketing?

Social media helps businesses connect with their audience, build brand awareness, and drive traffic through platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn.
